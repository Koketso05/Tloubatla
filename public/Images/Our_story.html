How we met

We met 11 years back in Pretoria “The Jacaranda City”when we were both tertiary students.


Lobola

On the 15th of July 2012 the Lamola family visited the Tloubatla family to start with the Lobola negotiations. On the 20th of October 2012 the Lobola negotiations were finalised and a Bridal Send Off Ceremony was held at Mmamoko’s home in Botlokwa.


Matrimony

On the 31st of March 2013 a Matrimonial ceremony was held at the Lutheran Church in Rosenkraanz Ga-Matlala;where we signed in the books of Home Affairs and Mmamoko was then the officially Mrs Lamola.

Our Blessing

On the 26th of June 2013 we were blessed with a healthy baby boy “Our Matsobane” Our bundle of Joy-Our reason for breathing
